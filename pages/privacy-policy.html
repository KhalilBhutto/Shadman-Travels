Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Shadman Travels & Tours ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use shadmantravels.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a flight enquiry, contact form, or "Get Notified" request on our website, we collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and (where provided) email address
  • Travel details you enter, such as departure/destination, travel dates, passenger count, travel class, preferred airline, and travel purpose
  • Basic technical information automatically collected by analytics tools, such as your approximate location, device type, and how you interact with our site (see Section 4)

We do not collect payment card information through this website. Any payment for bookings is arranged directly with our team over phone, WhatsApp, or in person.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your flight enquiry and provide fare quotations
  • To contact you by phone, WhatsApp, or email regarding your booking or enquiry
  • To process and manage your travel arrangements, including Umrah, Hajj, visa assistance, and tour packages
  • To send you a confirmation email acknowledging receipt of your enquiry, where you have provided an email address
  • To improve our website and services based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

3. Where Your Information Is Stored

Enquiry information you submit is stored securely in our internal customer relationship management system (Google Sheets, accessed only by authorized Shadman Travels & Tours staff) for the purpose of following up on your enquiry and maintaining booking records. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our website, so we can improve it. These tools may use cookies and collect anonymized or pseudonymized data such as pages visited, time on site, and general device/browser information. They do not collect your name, phone number, or email unless you separately submit that information through our forms. See our Cookie Policy for full details and how to manage cookie preferences.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 (traffic and conversion analytics)
  • Microsoft Clarity (session and usability analytics)
  • Google Sheets / Google Apps Script (secure internal lead and enquiry storage)
  • WhatsApp Business (for customer communication, if you contact us via WhatsApp)

6. Data Retention

We retain your enquiry and booking information for as long as necessary to provide our services and maintain accurate business and travel records, including for any legal, accounting, or regulatory requirements applicable to a licensed travel agency.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information held by us, subject to our legitimate business and legal record-keeping needs. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for use by adults arranging travel. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Passenger details for minors traveling with a booking party are provided by the adult making the booking.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal/regulatory reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
