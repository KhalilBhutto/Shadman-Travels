Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Shadman Travels & Tours uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on shadmantravels.com, and the choices available to you.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and allow us to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

2. Cookies We Use

ServicePurposeType

We do not use cookies for third-party advertising or ad retargeting on this website.

3. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how parts of the site function, such as analytics-driven improvements (it will not affect your ability to submit a flight enquiry or contact us). You can manage cookie preferences directly in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as our use of cookies and tracking technologies evolves. Please check back periodically for updates.
