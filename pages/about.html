Our Story

Founded in 2005, Shadman Travels & Tours has grown from a single Karachi office into one of the city's recognized names in air ticketing, Umrah and Hajj packages, visa assistance, and international tours. Over nearly two decades, we've helped tens of thousands of travelers reach destinations across the Middle East, Europe, the UK, North America, and the Asia-Pacific — with the same commitment to honest fares and reliable service that we started with.

What We Do

  • Airline Ticket Booking — domestic and international flights with 15+ airline partners
  • Umrah & Hajj Packages — guided, dependable arrangements for one of life's most meaningful journeys
  • Visa Assistance — support navigating visa requirements for your destination
  • International & Domestic Tours — tailored travel planning for leisure, business, and family trips
  • Travel Consultation — honest, expert advice from a team that's booked thousands of journeys

Why Travelers Choose Us

We're a government-licensed travel agency (License #3322) and an IATA-recognized agent, which means every booking we arrange goes through proper, accountable channels. Beyond the credentials, what keeps customers coming back — many for repeat bookings over the years — is straightforward: we answer the phone, we follow through, and we treat every traveler's plans as if they were our own family's.

Our Commitment

Your journey, our commitment — that's not just our tagline, it's how we operate. Whether you're booking a quick domestic flight or planning Umrah for your family, our team works to get you exclusive, guaranteed fares and clear answers, every step of the way.
